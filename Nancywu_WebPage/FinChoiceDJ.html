FinChoice System Overview

-1-Build a system(FinChoice), which hides all difficult financial tools behind, does not show difficult features in front of Simple User but transform into simple way to them.
-2-FinChoice Manager or Pro Users can test and modify existed strategies or upload their new strategies onto the system like Linear Regression Strategy in Stock trend predict function, or Max_Drawdown strategy in Risk assessment function or VaR strategy in Stock performance function or Similarity Strategy in Stock Recommendation function, or…

June 17, 2014

Interation with Pro/Simple Users

-1-Backend System, can be called strategies supplier system. Lots of Pro Users or managers create and do test on stock trading strategy.(Most of things can be done on Kibana Dashboard)
-2-Frontend System for Simple User. It doesn’t matter that simple users who do not understand strategies. Just know 2 things : 1) What is your interested stock performance during your chosen period like 90 days of period? 2) Which kind of stocks are similar with your interested stock
